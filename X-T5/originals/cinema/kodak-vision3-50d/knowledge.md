# Kodak Vision3 50D — Grade Analysis & Derivation

> Original (research + scan validated). See [`recipe.md`](recipe.md), the [cinema README](../README.md), and the [Knowledge base](../../../../Knowledge/README.md).

## The stock
Kodak Vision3 50D (5203/7203) is the slowest, finest-grained member of the Vision3 family — Kodak's own datasheet (archived: [`kodak-motion/vision3-50d-5203.pdf`](../../../_reference-sources/datasheets/README.md)) calls it "Kodak's finest grain" cinema stock, daylight-balanced (5500K), with the same "unrivaled highlight latitude" that defines the whole Vision3 line.

## Why it's on the "most notable and pleasing" list
Researched directly (not assumed): cinematographer discourse consistently singles this stock out. **Sean Baker's *Red Rocket*** used Vision3 daylight stocks for its raw, naturalistic exterior look. **Vanessa Whyte BSC** has publicly praised the Vision3 daylight family's exposure latitude — able to overexpose 3–4 stops outdoors and still hold highlight detail. 50D is the stock DPs reach for when the priority is *maximum cleanliness* in bright light — commercials, nature documentary, and any daylight exterior where grain must disappear.

## Real-scan confirmation (2 images, Wikimedia Commons, analysis-only)
1. **Bright clear-sky daylight** (ornate wooden house): grain essentially invisible even at full resolution; rich, accurate blue sky; warm wood tones rendered without colour cast; crisp fine detail in the fretwork trim.
2. **Overcast, flatter daylight** (rural path/treeline): grain remains clean under duller light too; natural, unshifted greens; neutral sky — confirms the stock doesn't need "help" from a colour cast to look right.

Both confirm the datasheet: this is the **cleanest, most neutral** stock in the family — no warmth to correct for, no grain to tame.

## The grade in one line
The cleanest possible daylight cinema image — invisible grain, neutral colour, extended highlight latitude, built for bright exteriors.

50D is the "as clean as cinema gets" anchor; 250D adds a touch of warmth and versatility for general daylight use; 500T is the night/interior specialist.
