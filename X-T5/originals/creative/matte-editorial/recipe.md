# Matte Editorial — Fujifilm X-T5 (X-Trans V) · STILLS

A **muted, faded, soft, airy** lifestyle-magazine look (Kinfolk / minimalist editorial): desaturated tones, **matte lifted blacks**, gentle highlights, a calm, expensive-looking restraint. Creative design (see [creative README](../README.md)); drop mood-board references into [`references/`](references/).

### Why
- Eterna: low-contrast, muted, rich-shadow cine base: the natural foundation for a soft matte look.
- Highlight −2 + Shadow −2: the signature **matte** curve: soft highlights *and* lifted, un-crushed blacks (nothing hits pure black).
- Color −2, CCE Weak: desaturated, calm, restrained palette.
- Clarity −2 + Sharpness −1: soft, dreamy, smooth mid-tones (the deliberate deviation for the airy softness).
- Expose +1/3 to +2/3: bright and high-key; overexposure enhances the faded, airy feel.

### Tuning
- **Warmer / cosier:** WB **+2R/−2B**. **Cooler / Scandi:** WB **−1R/+1B**.
- **More faded:** Shadow **−2**, Highlight **−2** with Exposure **+1**.
- **Video:** Eterna + WB + DR (manual) + Tone Curve + Sharpness transfer; Clarity/grain drop → keep Sharpness −1 and lean on the flat tone curve; a mist filter adds the soft matte feel on video.
