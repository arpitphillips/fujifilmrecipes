# Mumbai Monsoon — Fujifilm X-T5 (X-Trans V) · STILLS

An **original** recipe designed for Mumbai monsoon conditions: flat overcast light, wet reflective streets, rain haze, rain-deepened greens, and bright colour accents (umbrellas, black-&-yellow taxis, signage) against a grey backdrop, often in low light. It's a cinematic **cool-shadow / warm-accent** grade that adds depth to flat light while keeping the moody, wet atmosphere. Rationale in [`knowledge.md`](knowledge.md).

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Film Simulation | Classic Negative |
| Grain Effect | Strong, Large |
| Color Chrome Effect | Strong |
| Color Chrome FX Blue | Weak |
| White Balance | 6500K, +2 Red & −2 Blue |
| Dynamic Range | DR400 |
| Highlight (Tone Curve) | −2 |
| Shadow (Tone Curve) | +1 |
| Color | +1 |
| Sharpness | −1 |
| High ISO NR | −4 |
| Clarity | 0 |
| ISO | Auto, up to ISO 12800 |
| Exposure Compensation | −1/3 to +1/3 (judge per scene) |

### Why these values (short version)
- **Classic Negative** — its *hard tonality* injects contrast/depth into flat overcast light, and its cool teal-leaning shadows suit wet streets and rain.
- **DR400 + Highlight −2** — grey monsoon skies clip easily; this protects them with a soft roll-off, and lifts shadow detail in dark scenes.
- **Shadow +1** — moody, slightly deepened blacks without crushing rain/wet-street detail.
- **6500K, +2R/−2B** — neutralises the blue-grey overcast and adds a touch of warmth to skin and colour accents, giving the cinematic teal-shadow / warm-highlight split against Classic Neg's cool shadows.
- **Color +1, CCE Strong, CCFXB Weak** — keeps colour restrained/moody overall but lets accents pop, deepens blues/teals of wet surfaces and rain.
- **Grain Strong/Large, Sharpness −1, Clarity 0** — atmospheric, humid, filmic texture from grain + soft edges (Clarity kept at 0 per the quality standard; add a physical mist filter for more haze rather than negative Clarity).
- **ISO up to 12800** — monsoon days are dark; the raised ceiling handles low light (grain masks the noise).

### Tuning for the day
- **Heavy rain / very moody:** Shadow **+2**, Color **0** — deeper, greyer, more melancholic.
- **Colour-pop street (taxis, umbrellas):** Color **+2**, Clarity **0** — livelier accents.
- **Blue-hour / night rain with neon:** drop to **5500K** and let the practicals glow; consider the [CineStill 800T](../../reference-recipes/cinestill-800t/recipe.md) recipe instead for full neon-night work.
