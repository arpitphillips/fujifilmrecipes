# Kodachrome 64 — Fujifilm X-T5 (X-Trans V)

Classic Chrome base. Emulates 1970s-era Kodachrome slide film, the site's long-time #1 or #2 most popular recipe. Great all-rounder, especially in daylight.

[Source](https://fujixweekly.com/2022/11/28/kodachrome-64-fujifilm-x-t5-x-trans-v-film-simulation-recipe/)
