Community recipes

The recipes on this shelf were made by other people. I keep them here to test my own originals against the best of what the community has published, and because some of them are simply excellent. Every card names its creator, and every page links to the original publication. If one of these becomes your daily look, go tell the person who made it.

The regulars: Fuji X Weekly (Ritchie Roesch), Ross McConaghy and Luís Costa.
