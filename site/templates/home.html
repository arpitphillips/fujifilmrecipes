How this works

A recipe is a bundle of in-camera JPEG settings — film simulation, white-balance shift, dynamic range, tone curve, grain and friends — saved into one of the X-T5's C1–C7 custom presets. Dial it in once, and the camera bakes the look into every frame at the moment you press the shutter. No editing. No presets to buy. No "I'll fix it later" folder.

New to the dials themselves? Start with the film-simulation bases and the white-balance shift grid — twenty minutes of reading that will change how you shoot.
