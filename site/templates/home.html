How to use a recipe

Each recipe is a bundle of in-camera JPEG settings — film simulation, white-balance shift, dynamic range, tone curve, grain and more — programmed into one of the X-T5's C1–C7 custom presets (Image Quality → Edit/Save Custom Setting). The camera bakes the look in at capture time; there is no editing step.

New to the settings themselves? Start with the film-simulation bases and the white-balance shift grid.
